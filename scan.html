CloudFlare Scan

Scan your website for malicious items like malware, phishing attempts, and more.

CloudFlare Scan is a new service app that alerts CloudFlare customers to dozens of different web infections including malware and phishing attempts on their website. Advanced Scan takes this up a notch by also providing a crawler that will check every URL on your website and check it against our sources to see if the link is blacklisted anywhere else.

Check back!

In the first 24 hours after adding a domain to CloudFlare, Scan won't have any information to share yet. This module serves to inform users and provide them with a link to a knowledge base concerning the app.

This illustration reassures new users that Scan is working properly and they will have access shortly. Instead of placing boring, plain text here, I created an illustration and module containing helpful information to assist and engage users.

Clear of malware

Even when no malware is found and your site is free and clear of any threats, we'll still you. CloudFlare will share how many items were scanned and when, and provide you with descriptions of the features, and settings that can be adjusted.

Threats found

Threats aren't found often, but when they are, Scan will tell you. CloudFlare Scan provides a table of specific URLs where malware was found on your site, or where you were found linking to an off-site blacklisted page.

When a user chooses to review a infection, they open a modal containing details of the URL where the infection was found, and which of our sources picked it up. The user is also directed to a help article so they can learn how to remove the infection and resolve the issue.
