CloudFlare Scan

CloudFlare Scan is a service app that alerts CloudFlare customers to dozens of different web infections—including malware and phishing attempts—on their website.

Process

Initially our product team planned to include illustrations within the Scan UI. Ultimately we decided to elimiate these elements—Scan is a serious application, and we chose to create a simpler UI.

No threats found

Almost 100% of the time a CloudFlare customer site will be free of Malware. When no malware is found and your site is free and clear of any threats, we don't just tell you that you're safe. We'll let you know when we last scanned and offer the option to increase your scan frequency by upgrading your plan level.

In addition, users can turn on Advanced Scan. Advance Scan improves your security by utilizing a CloudFlare crawler that will search every URL on your website and check it against our sources to see if the link is blacklisted anywhere else.

Threats found

Threats aren't found often, but when they are, Scan will tell you. CloudFlare Scan provides a table of specific URLs where malware was found on your site, or where you were found linking to an off-site blacklisted page.

When a user chooses to review a infection, a modal is opened containing details of the URL where the infection was found, and which of our sources picked it up. Even if CloudFlare didn't find the infection, we'll tell you who did and how to fix it.
