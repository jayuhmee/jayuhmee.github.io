Legos

Having been tasked with providing artwork for CloudFlare's London office, we decided to put together Lego sets to provide not only artwork, but also a team building activity for the UK staff. I drew from old CloudFlare shirt designs, popular product logos, and some inside jokes to design the artwork.

After the lego artwork had been designed, I created instructions for how each piece should be built, and placed an order. Nearly 2,000 legos were used to complete the artwork and our London team got to spend an afternoon building Legos together.
