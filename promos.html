Promos

Shirts

I've created a variety of shirts for CloudFlare; for large partner events, for customer contests and giveaways, and for employees — just because. Like most San Francisco offices, we love our t-shirts, and I love creating things for our team to enjoy.

Legos

Having been tasked with providing artwork for CloudFlare's London office, I decided to put together Lego sets to provide not only artwork, but also a team building activity for the UK staff. I drew from old CloudFlare shirt designs, popular product logos, and some inside jokes to design the artwork.

After the lego artwork had been designed, I created instructions for how each piece should be built, and placed an order. Nearly 2,000 legos were used to complete the artwork.
